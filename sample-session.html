What to Expect

Sample Sessions

Every session is tailored to your player's level and goals. Here's what a typical 40-minute session looks like.

← Back to Home
Beginner

Foundation Builder

40 minutes · Ages 8–11

0–5 min

Warm-Up & Check-In

Dynamic stretches, ball handling basics, and a quick chat about goals for the day.

5–15 min

Ball Handling Fundamentals

Stationary dribbling, crossovers, and control drills. Building comfort with both hands.

15–25 min

Shooting Form

BEEF technique (Balance, Eyes, Elbow, Follow-through). Close-range form shooting.

25–35 min

1-on-1 Moves

Basic triple threat, jab steps, and simple attack moves. Learning to read the defender.

35–40 min

Game & Cool Down

Fun competitive drill or mini-game. Recap what we learned and set homework.

Intermediate

Game Ready

40 minutes · Ages 11–14

0–5 min

Dynamic Warm-Up

Ball handling combos on the move. Getting the body and mind game-ready.

5–15 min

Advanced Ball Handling

Speed dribbling, combo moves, and handling under pressure. Two-ball drills.

15–25 min

Scoring Moves

Pull-up jumpers, floaters, finishing through contact. Reading the defense.

25–35 min

Live Situations

1-on-1 from different spots. Decision making: when to shoot, drive, or pass.

35–40 min

Competitive Finish

Game-pressure drill with consequences. Mental toughness and clutch moments.

Sessions are customized based on your player's strengths, weaknesses, and what they want to work on. No two sessions are exactly alike.
